    • Rules of the Game

  • The main objective of the game is to keep your job. This is done by keeping your wage above the threshold.
  • To increase your wage your wage you must complete orders. This is done by catching each food and drink order as they fall down the screen, then bringing the order to the table.
  • For each order you bring out, your wage goes up, for each order you don’t bring out, your wage goes down.
  • When an order is completed, a table will appear at the side of screen. The player must then navigate Wally to collide with the table. If Wally reaches the table, then the player’s wage will then be increased. However, if another food or drink item lands on Wally or if Wally slips on spillage, the sprite for Wally falling will be activated and the player’s wage will be reduced by €10.
  • If the player accidently catches more items, then are on the order while delivering an order, then the falling sprite will be activated, and the players wage will go down.
  • When a player reaches the table then there next order will appear with more items to collect then the previous order. The game is on a timer which last for 3 minutes. The game will end when the countdown hits 0 or if the player wage reaches 0.
  • The highest score is measured by the highest wage at the end of the game.
