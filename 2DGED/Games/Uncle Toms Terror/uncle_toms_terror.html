    Meet Your Icons

    Across the park,our guests will be given the chance to meet there favourite heroes from across Disney.Some of our must popular characters are listed below.

    Mickey Mouse

    Affilation:Walt Disney
    Species:Mouse
    Gender:Male

    Mickey Mouse is the main icon of our park. In our park, he is all in green to represent Ireland. You can find our Mickey Mouse mascot in the main park. If you see him, be quick to get a photo with him. He may need your help in finding his lost treasure!

    Minnie Mouse

    Affilation:Walt Disney
    Species:Mouse
    Gender:Female

    Minnie Mouse is Mickey Mouse's girlfriend. She keeps him in check. You can find Minnie in the same place as Mickey in Disneyland Park. Make sure to get a photo seperate with Mickey nd Minnie because they might be too busy with eachother!

    Donald Duck

    Affilation:Walt Disney
    Species:Duck
    Gender:Male

    Quack! Quack! Donald Duck is a iconic character from Disney and his mascot can be found in Disneyland Park. The Donald Duck mascot is extremely friendly and will gladly take photos with guests. Coming soon to our park is Daisy Duck who will soon steal Donald Duck's thunder.

    Pluto

    Affilation:Walt Disney
    Species:Dog
    Gender:Male

    Disney's fan favourite pet "Pluto" can be found lurking around in our main park. He is usually close in hand beside his owner, Snoopie. Be careful when taking a photo with the Pluto mascot that he doesn't try to lick you!

    Woody

    Affilation:Toy Story
    Species:Toy
    Gender:Male

    The cowboy from the "Wild Wide West", Woody is a fan favourite from the Toy Story Films. His story was concluded recently "Toy Story 4". Woodie's mascot can be found in Disneyland Studios near the RC Racer ride.

    Buzz Lightyear

    Affilation:Toy Story
    Species:Toy
    Gender:Male

    Woodie's loyal companion "Buzz ligtyear" is an iconic character for his hilarious space man impression and the heart warming moments with him and Woodie. Buzz lightyear can be found in Disneyland Park beside his ride "Buzz lightyear Laser Blast".

    Chewbacca

    Affilation:Star Wars
    Species:Wookie
    Gender:Male

    The Wookie giant, Chewbacca is the loyal companion of Han Solo from Star Wars. When taking a photo with the mascot make sure to give him a hug becuase his fur is incredible soft. Don't bother trying to communicate to Chewbacca because you will get the response of "RAHHHHH".

    Darth Vader

    Affilation:Star Wars
    Species:Part man, part robot
    Gender:Male programming

    Darth Vader is a villian from Star Wars and probably the most famous villian of all time. His heavy breathing is haunting and fills the people in the room with fear. You must go and find his throne in Disneyland Studios and take a photo beside him and embrace the dark side.

    Mushu

    Affilation:Mulan
    Species:Dragon
    Gender:Male

    The talkative dragon from the original "Mulan" film is a hilarious characters who helps Mulan on her journey. For a dragon he is quite small, but that is apart of the joke. Mushu can be found in our "Mulan" restaurant. Make sure to get a photo with him fast as he is a popular character.

    Baymax

    Affilation:Big Hero 6
    Species:Robot
